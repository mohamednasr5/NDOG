❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NileDogs, NDOG, mining, staking, and more.

What is NileDogs (NDOG)?

NileDogs (NDOG) is a community-driven cryptocurrency reward platform. Users mine NDOG daily by claiming rewards, complete missions, play mini-games, stake their tokens for up to 120% APR, and earn through a 3-level referral program. The token has a fixed supply of 100 billion.

Is NileDogs free to join?

Yes. Signing up is completely free — just sign in with Google. You can start mining NDOG immediately and earn through referrals, missions, and airdrops without ever spending money.

How does daily mining work?

Once signed in, click the "Claim" button on your dashboard every 24 hours to mine NDOG. Each claim credits tokens directly to your balance. Streaks earn bonus multipliers — the more consecutive days you claim, the more you earn.

What happens if I miss a daily claim?

If you miss a 24-hour window, your streak resets to 1. You can still claim the next day, but you'll lose any streak bonus multiplier. Founder NFT pass holders get one streak-protect per month.

Are there VIP or Founder bonuses?

Yes. Founder NFT pass holders receive a 2x multiplier on every claim. VIP tiers (1-5) are earned through staking volume and unlock multipliers from 1.1x to 1.5x on daily claims and missions.

What are the staking options and APRs?

NDOG can be staked for 30, 90, 180, or 365 days. The longer you lock, the higher the APR — up to 120% on the 365-day tier. APRs are dynamic and reduce as the staking pool fills. See the Staking page for current rates.

Can I unstake early?

Yes, but early unstake incurs a 20% penalty on accrued rewards (principal is returned in full). The penalty is returned to the staking pool to reward long-term holders. There is no penalty if you wait until maturity.

How does the 3-level referral program work?

When someone signs up using your referral link and completes their first daily claim, you earn 50 NDOG (Level 1). When they refer someone, you earn 20 NDOG (Level 2). When that person refers someone, you earn 10 NDOG (Level 3). There is no cap on referral earnings.

Can I refer myself with multiple accounts?

No. Self-referral and multi-accounting are strictly prohibited and detected via device fingerprinting, IP analysis, and behavior patterns. Violating accounts are banned and balances forfeited. See our Terms of Service.

How does NileDogs prevent fraud and bots?

We use device fingerprinting, VPN/proxy detection, rate limiting, IP velocity checks, behavioral analysis, and reCAPTCHA. Every claim, mission completion, and referral is run through our anti-fraud engine before tokens are credited.

What data do you collect about me?

We collect your email, display name, profile photo (from Google), country (from IP), device fingerprint, and usage analytics (claims, missions, stakes). We do NOT collect payment information. See our full Privacy Policy.

What is the total supply of NDOG?

100,000,000,000 NDOG (100 billion). The supply is fixed and cannot be increased. Distribution: 40% mining, 20% staking, 15% airdrop, 10% team, 8% liquidity, 4% marketing, 3% reserve. See Tokenomics.

When will NDOG be listed on exchanges?

We plan a DEX listing in Q1–Q2 2026 (Phase 3 of our roadmap), followed by Tier-2 and Tier-1 CEX listings in Q3 2026+. Exchange selection depends on community growth, security audits, and partnership terms. Subscribe to News for announcements.

Is NDOG a good investment? Will the price go up?

NDOG is a community token, not financial advice and not a guaranteed investment. The price of any cryptocurrency can go up or down. We make no promises about future value. Only participate with tokens you can afford to hold or lose.

How do I contact support?

The fastest way to reach us is via our Telegram community or our contact form. For business inquiries, email contact@ndogcoin.com.
