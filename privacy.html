1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Email address — collected when you sign in with Google.
  • Display name — your name as provided by Google or as you edit it on your profile.
  • Photo URL — your Google profile picture URL.
  • Contact form submissions — name, email, subject, and message you submit via our Contact page.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Country — inferred from your IP address at sign-in for country leaderboards.
  • Device fingerprint — a hashed identifier derived from browser and device characteristics, used for fraud prevention (multi-account detection, bot blocking).
  • Usage analytics — pages visited, buttons clicked, sessions, claim frequency, mission completion, and other interactions with the Service.
  • Technical data — IP address, browser type and version, operating system, screen resolution, referrer URL, and timestamps.
  • Authentication tokens — Firebase ID tokens and refresh tokens required to keep you signed in.

1.3 Transactional Data

  • Claims — daily mining claims, streak counts, and bonus multipliers.
  • Mission progress — which missions you've started or completed.
  • Staking contracts — amounts, durations, APRs, and accrued rewards.
  • Referral tree — your referrer (if any) and the users you've referred (across 3 levels).
  • Balance and transactions — your NDOG balance and full credit/debit history.

1.4 Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or store: payment card numbers, bank account details, government IDs, biometric data, your private crypto wallet keys, or your physical address. We never ask for your wallet seed phrase — anyone who does is a scammer.

2. Firebase Services We Use

The NileDogs platform is built on Google Firebase. Specifically, we use:

  • Firebase Authentication — for Google sign-in and session management.
  • Firebase Realtime Database — for storing user profiles, balances, transactions, claims, missions, staking contracts, referrals, leaderboards, and news.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) — for push notifications about claims, mission resets, and announcements.
  • Firebase Analytics — for aggregate usage metrics and funnel analysis.
  • Firebase Hosting — for serving the website and static assets.

All Firebase services are governed by Google's Privacy and Security practices. Your data is stored on Google Cloud infrastructure, with regional replication.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Service (claims, staking, missions, leaderboards);
  • Authenticate you and keep your session secure;
  • Detect and prevent fraud, multi-accounting, bots, and abuse;
  • Calculate and credit NDOG rewards, streaks, and bonuses;
  • Generate global, country, weekly, monthly, and all-time leaderboards;
  • Send you notifications about your account, missions, and platform announcements;
  • Respond to your support requests and contact form submissions;
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the Service;
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

The Service uses cookies and browser local storage for the following purposes:

  • Authentication cookies — keep you signed in across sessions.
  • Theme cookie (ndog_theme) — remembers your light/dark preference.
  • Language cookie (ndog_lang) — remembers your selected language (EN or AR).
  • Local storage — caches translation strings and mission data to reduce network requests.
  • Service Worker cache — enables offline access to previously-loaded pages (PWA support).

You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features (session persistence, theme memory) will not work correctly.

5. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Sign-In & Firebase — authentication and database. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — font delivery (Inter, Plus Jakarta Sans, Cairo, JetBrains Mono).
  • reCAPTCHA — bot detection on sign-in and form submission.
  • Telegram, X (Twitter), YouTube — social link redirects to our official channels.

We do not sell your data to any third party.

6. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers — Firebase and other infrastructure providers who help us operate the Service, bound by their own privacy commitments.
  • For legal compliance — if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.
  • Aggregate / anonymized data — we may publish anonymous statistics (total users, total NDOG mined, daily claims) for transparency.
  • Business transfers — in the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, user data may be transferred to the successor entity with the same protections.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data:

  • All Firebase Realtime Database access is governed by security rules — users can only read/write their own data, except for public leaderboards and news.
  • Database rules enforce server-side validation and rate limits.
  • HTTPS / TLS encryption is enforced for all traffic.
  • Admin actions are logged and require role-based access (admin or mod).
  • Device fingerprinting and IP velocity checks detect suspicious behavior in real time.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we work hard to protect your information.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will:

  • Delete your profile, balance, and personal identifiers within 30 days.
  • Retain anonymized transaction records (for ledger integrity) — these cannot be linked back to you.
  • Retain fraud logs and banned-user records for 12 months for security.
  • Retain contact form submissions for 90 days for support follow-up.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your account and data.
  • Right to restrict processing — limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object — opt out of certain processing, such as analytics.
  • Right to withdraw consent — at any time, by deleting your account.

To exercise any of these rights, email contact@ndogcoin.com with the subject "Privacy Request". We will respond within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own, including the United States and European Union, where our infrastructure (Google Cloud / Firebase) is hosted. We rely on standard contractual clauses and Google's compliance frameworks to ensure appropriate safeguards for international transfers.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting an announcement on the News page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. Continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
