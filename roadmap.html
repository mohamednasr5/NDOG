🗺️ Roadmap 2025-2027

From community build to global exchange listings and cross-chain governance.

✓
Phase 1
Q1 – Q2 2025

Foundation & Launch

Building the core platform, authentication, daily mining, and the 3-level referral engine.

  • ✅ Platform launch (web app)
  • ✅ Google authentication & One Tap
  • ✅ Daily mining & streak system
  • ✅ 3-level referral program (50/20/10 NDOG)
  • ✅ Anti-fraud engine (device fingerprinting, VPN detection)
  • ✅ Firebase Realtime Database backend
  • ✅ English & Arabic i18n
●
Phase 2
Q3 – Q4 2025

Earning Expansion

Adding staking, missions, mini-games, and global leaderboards to deepen engagement.

  • 🔄 Staking (30/90/180/365 days, up to 120% APR)
  • 🔄 Daily, weekly, monthly missions
  • 🔄 Lucky Wheel, Lucky Box, Scratch Card, Treasure Hunt
  • 🔄 6 leaderboards (global, country, referral, weekly, monthly, all-time)
  • 🔄 Airdrop center with social tasks
  • 🔄 Block explorer for transactions
  • 🔄 Mobile responsive + PWA

Phase 3
Q1 – Q2 2026

Liquidity & Reach

Bringing NDOG to decentralized exchanges and mobile, with full multi-language support.

  • 🔜 DEX listing (Uniswap / PancakeSwap)
  • 🔜 Native mobile app (iOS + Android)
  • 🔜 Multi-language expansion (FR, ES, TR, RU, ZH, HI, UR, ID)
  • 🔜 On-chain transaction anchoring
  • 🔜 Public API & SDK
  • 🔜 Founder NFT pass (multiplier perks)
  • 🔜 Partnerships with regional crypto communities

Phase 4
Q3 2026 – Q4 2027

Decentralization & Scale

CEX listings, NFT collection, governance DAO, and cross-chain expansion.

  • 🔜 Tier-2 and Tier-1 CEX listings
  • 🔜 NFT collection launch (NileDogs Genesis Pack)
  • 🔜 Governance DAO (NDOG-staked voting)
  • 🔜 Cross-chain bridges (Ethereum, BNB, Polygon, Solana, TON)
  • 🔜 Decentralized staking with on-chain smart contracts
  • 🔜 Open-source treasury dashboard
  • 🔜 Annual pack summit (virtual + IRL)
