1. Overview

NileDogs (NDOG) is a community-driven crypto rewards platform launching on January 1, 2028. Members earn NDOG tokens through daily claims, referrals, tasks, spin-wheels, lucky boxes, and leaderboard achievements.

The platform emphasizes accessibility, transparency, and fair economics — no private sales, no hidden allocations, no founder pre-mines. Every claim, referral, and achievement builds toward the mainnet launch and token exchange listing.

Key Highlights

  • 1 Billion NDOG supply (fixed).
  • Pre-launch Phase: Earn loyalty points, early adopter badges, founder status, and priority on exchange listings.
  • No Wallet Required: Sign in with Google; tokens migrate to mainnet wallets on January 1, 2028.
  • Three-Tier Referral System: Earn from direct referrals, their referrals, and third-level referrals.
  • VIP Tiers & Leaderboards: Compete for weekly/monthly rewards and exclusive community perks.
  • Fraud Prevention: Device fingerprinting, rate-limiting, server-side validation.

3. Core Mechanics

3.1 Daily Claims

Every user claims 10 NDOG/day as a base amount. Claims are multiplied by:

  • Streak Bonus: Up to 3.0× for consecutive daily claims (max 30 days).
  • Referral Bonus: Up to 1.25× based on active referral count.
  • Founder Badge: +50% multiplier (permanent) for pre-launch adopters.

Theoretical daily max: 10 × 3.0 × 1.25 × 1.5 = 56 NDOG/day.

3.2 Spin Wheel

Users spin once per day to win bonus NDOG. Outcomes range from +5 NDOG (common) to +100 NDOG (rare). Spin results are server-validated to prevent manipulation.

3.3 Lucky Box

Every 6 hours, users can open a lucky box for a chance at bonus tokens (+10 to +50 NDOG) or streak extensions. Encourages engagement without overwhelming rewards.

3.4 Leaderboards

Global and regional leaderboards track top earners weekly and monthly. Top 10 earners each period receive exclusive badges and bonus payouts.

3.5 Tasks & Missions

Periodic community tasks (e.g., "Share on Twitter," "Join Discord," "Refer 5 friends") unlock bonus NDOG. Tasks promote organic growth and engagement.

5. Rewards & Early Adopter Program

5.2 Reward Tiers

Five tiers — Bronze, Silver, Gold, Diamond, Legendary — unlock by reaching cumulative balance thresholds. Higher tiers unlock exclusive events, bonus quests, and limited-time multipliers.

6. Security & Fraud Prevention

6.1 Authentication

Secure OAuth 2.0 via Google Sign-In. Session persistence keeps users logged in across browser restarts. One-click logout available.

6.2 Multi-Account Prevention

On first login, the client generates a device fingerprint combining user agent, language, screen resolution, timezone, and a random token stored in localStorage. If a different user ID attempts to reuse the same fingerprint, the account is flagged for admin review.

6.3 Rate Limiting

  • Daily Claim: 1 per 24 hours per account.
  • Spin Wheel: 1 per 24 hours per account.
  • Lucky Box: 1 per 6 hours per account.
  • Referral Assignment: 1 per new user (immutable).

6.4 Admin Review System

Flagged accounts appear in the admin dashboard. Admins can suspend, penalize, or delete accounts that violate community rules or exploit the reward system.

6.5 Data Privacy

NileDogs collects only data returned by Google (name, email, profile picture), plus timezone and device fingerprint to prevent fraud. No third-party analytics or ad networks. User data is never shared.

6.6 Transport Security

All communications use HTTPS. Service workers cache only static assets; live data is always fetched fresh for accuracy and security.

8. Governance

During pre-launch, governance is centralized with the founding team for rapid iteration. After the January 2028 TGE, NileDogs transitions to community governance where NDOG holders propose and vote on:

  • Reward parameter adjustments (base claims, multipliers, referral tiers).
  • New task types and event formats.
  • Partnership allocations from the reserve.
  • Future chain migrations and technical upgrades.

Voting weight is determined by NDOG balance and founder status, with a quadratic component to prevent whale dominance.

9. Legal & Compliance

9.1 Nature of NDOG (Pre-Launch)

During pre-launch, NDOG is a loyalty point redeemable for in-app benefits, early access, and reputation. It is not a security, debt instrument, or financial contract. Token migration post-launch will comply with applicable jurisdictions.

9.2 User Eligibility

Users must be 13+ with a valid Google account. Users in sanctioned jurisdictions are not permitted.

9.3 Privacy

NileDogs collects only data returned by Google at sign-in (name, email, photo) plus timezone and device fingerprint. No third-party cookies, analytics SDKs, or ads.

9.4 Account Termination

Admins may suspend or delete accounts that violate community rules, attempt to exploit the reward system, or create multi-accounts from the same device. Terminated users forfeit unclaimed rewards.

9.5 Disclaimer

NileDogs is provided "as-is" without warranty. The team is not liable for lost rewards due to service interruptions, account suspension, or future protocol changes.
